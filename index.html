You Are Not Alone

No matter what pain you experience in life- know that it is temporary.
Don't ever think suicide is an option- it's a permanent solution to a temporary problem.
If you are struggling with suicidal thoughts, REACH OUT.

Even though it doesn't always feel that way, remember that:
You are loved.
You are cared for.

I promise you, happiness will come your way. Everything takes time.
